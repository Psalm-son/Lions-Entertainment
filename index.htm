About Lions Records

Lions Entertainment is a recording studio owned by Startup Lions Talent Hub, located near the shores of Lake Turkana. It serves as a creative space where talented individuals develop and refine their skills in poetry, songwriting, and rapping. Dedicated to discovering and nurturing talent, Lions Entertainment empowers artists to make a global impact with their voices.
